System Specification

The Void

01 // Origin_Intent

VOID was conceptualized as a transition point between terrestrial limitation and multiplanetary reality. It is not a news portal, but a visual archive of humanity's progress into the heliosphere.

By aggregating real-time telemetry from Artemis II, historical data from the Voyager missions, and interactive visualizers from NASA's deep space network, we provide a unified "Heads-Up Display" for the modern explorer.
